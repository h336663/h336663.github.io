Home

ICIER has her own ~ 2000-m2 office-laboratory suite, equipped with state-of-art facilities and laboratory equipment, and is located in Modern Engineering Plaza in Xianlin (仙林) campus (the main campus of Nanjing University), a separate building from the building that hosts the College of Earth Sciences.

Core Identity

International Center for Isotope Effects Research (ICIER) explores isotope effects and applies mass-spectrometry and spectroscopy to fundamental problems in the dynamics and evolution of Earth, planets, and life.

We

We are a group of independent-minded scientists who work on diverse scientific fields but share a common interest in isotope effects at molecular level.

Isotope effects

Isotope effects are the relative difference in rate of reaction between different isotopes of an element or isotopologues of a molecule. The difference reveals information on reaction kinetics at higher dimensions than changes in chemical concentrations could.

Organization

ICIER is a cross-college and cross-discipline research hub. It is currently based on and growing out of the College of Earth Sciences and Geology Engineering in Nanjing University.

read more

All our faculty members are affiliated with and recruit and train students through individual colleges in Nanjing University, including but not limited to Earth Sciences and Geology Engineering, Planetary Sciences, Chemistry, Environmental Sciences, Geography and Oceanography, Atmospheric Sciences, Medicine, and Life Sciences. Both Chinese and English are ICIER’s working languages.

Lab Services

We analyze the common isotope compositions δD, δ13C, δ15N, δ18O, and δ34S of diverse organic or inorganic materials. In addition, OAISC is specialized in Δ17O analysis (natural or doped). Please see our analytical methods and precision on the back. We also provide reference materials and services on sample extraction and purification, data interpretation, and training.
